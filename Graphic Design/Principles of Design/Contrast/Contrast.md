
### What is Contrast?
- the differences or opposite of two elements in a design.

#### Types of Contrast
- size/scale
- color/value
- form
- shapes
- texture
- typography
- alignment/direction
- [[movement]]

### Importance of Contrast
- Creates Emphasis
- Creates [[Visual Hierarchy]]
- Creates [[Movement]]
- Creates Visual Interest
